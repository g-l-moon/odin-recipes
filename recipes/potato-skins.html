Potato Skins

Description

Crispy potato skins, cooked with a parika glaze and filled with gorgonzola and pancetta.

Ingredients

  • 2 large baking potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 125g pancetta
  • 3 tbsp double cream
  • 125g gorgonzola
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C.
  2. Prick the potatoes with a fork and place on the top shelf of the oven.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until tender (about an hour).
  4. Cut the potatoes in half and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  5. Preheat the grill, and line the rack with tinfoil.
  6. Mix the oil and paprika, and use half to brush the outside of the potato skins.
  7. Place the glazed potato skins under the grill for 3 minutes or until crisp
  8. Heat the remaining paprika and oil, and use it to fry the pancetta.
  9. Mix the potato flesh, fried pancetta, gorgonzola and parsley
  10. Add this mixture to the potato skins and cook for a further 15 minutes.

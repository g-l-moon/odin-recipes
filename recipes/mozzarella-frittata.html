Mozzarella Frittata

Description

Mozzarella frittata, with a tomato and basil salad.

Ingredients

  • 6 tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 7 eggs
  • 325g mozzarella
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes very thin,tear the basil leaves and place over the tomatoes.
  2. For the dressing, whisk the olive oil, lemon juice and sugar together. Season to tast with black pepper.
  3. Preheat the grill.
  4. Beat the eggs together with salt.
  5. Grate the mozzarella and combine with the eggs and spring onion.
  6. Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the egg mixture.
  7. Cook for 4-6 minutes until the frittata is golden-brown and firm.
  8. Place under the grill for 4-5 minutes.
  9. Serve with the tomato and basil salad.

Thai Fish Cakes

Description

Thai-style cod and prawn fish cakes with chilli for some added kick

Ingredients

  • 1 deseeded red chilli pepper
  • 4 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 2 spring onions
  • lemon grass
  • 75g prawns
  • 275g deboned cod fillet
  • sweet chilli dipping sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C.
  2. Blend the chilli, coriander, garlic, spring onions and lemon grass in a food processor.
  3. Dry the prawns and cod with kitchen paper.
  4. Add the cod and prawns to the food processor and roughly blend.
  5. Season to taste and blend again.
  6. Dampen hands and shape the mixture into roughly twelve discs.
  7. Place the discs on a baking sheet, and cook for 15 minutes or until cooked completely through.
  8. Serve with sweet chilli sauce.
